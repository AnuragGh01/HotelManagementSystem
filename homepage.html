About


"BonVoyage", a Hotel Management System or HMS, is an application that will help users better utilize rooms used by customers and other guests. HMS helps users manage guest flows by affording them the ability to easily check guests in, check them out, and generate stay reports, among other things.Interactive GUI and the ability to manage various hotel bookings and rooms make this system very flexible and convenient.
